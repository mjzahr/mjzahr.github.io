CME001: ICME Refresher Course

ICME Track - Partial Differential Equations

Lecture

Building 530, Room 127
September 16 - 19, 2013
9a - 12p

Office Hours

Durand 028
By appointment

Intended Audience

This course is intended to provide incoming ICME students a refresher on the Fourier Transform and a primer on numerical methods for PDEs. This will be useful background for CME 303 (Partial Differential Equations of Applied Mathematics) and CME 306 (Numerical Solution of Partial Differential Equations).

Topics

  • Fourier Transform

  • Introduction to Numerical PDEs
